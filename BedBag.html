What is the BEDBAG?

What if everyone could carry their bed with them ?

Product

Personalization of the BedBag to adapt it to each person and each event

Company

the project is in the process of maturing and currently Mrs ADRAR Samia is alone in the project accompanied by the University of Perpignan and PMM (Perpignan Méditerranée Métropole), not yet made a decision regarding the status of the company as to the possible appearance of a partner during .

The idea

The project involves the development of a portable and comfortable bed, able to be transported anywhere easily while having a comfort similar to a real bed.

Why to choose the BEDBAG?

Quite simply because it has several advantages.

  • Portable and durable

    Because its weight less than 4 Kg and it is foldable The BedBag will be a bed made of recyclable material mainly PVC and its lifespan 2-3 years

  • easy and fast installation

    Does not require tools for installation or storage. Quick installation, in less than 5 minutes without effort.

  • Transition to Full Service

    With low environmental impact. Comfortable It has the same properties as a real bed

About

ADRAR SAMIA

The BEDBAG project is led by myself Samia ADRAR graduated from a Master EEEA at UPVD, currently in the first year of a thesis in electronics at the IETR laboratory in Nantes. My project concerns the development of a portable and comfortable bed. This idea came to me a long time ago on a boat trip. Following a reservation of last minute, I spent 18 hours sitting on a chair in a boat. Impossible to sleep. Looking around I realized that the problem was shared, a lot passengers slept on the floor, others like me had a sleepless night. It's in meeting the eyes of a mother with her child that I clicked: "if only everyone could carry their bed with them ”? I unveiled the BedBag project for the first time during my participation in the startup weekend in Perpignan from October 13 to 15, 2017. I was able to validate the relevance of the project there thanks to the advice and the encouragement of the juries of the competition who awarded him the third prize of the Start-up weekend. .
